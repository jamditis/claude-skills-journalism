Understanding FOIA landscape

Federal FOIA

5 U.S.C. § 552

Covers executive branch agencies. 20 business day response deadline. 9 exemptions.

State laws

Varies by state

All 50 states have public records laws. Exemptions and deadlines vary widely.

Local records

Often covered by state law

Municipal, county, and school board records. Usually fastest turnaround.

Drafting effective requests

Do this

  • • Be specific but not too narrow: "Emails between [Office] and [External Party] regarding [Topic] from [Date Range]"
  • • Use agency terminology: Research how they categorize information
  • • Define your terms: Specify what you mean by "communications"
  • • Request specific record types: Emails, texts, memos, contracts, datasets

Avoid this

  • • Too broad: "All documents about climate change"
  • • Too narrow: "The email from John Smith on March 15, 2024 at 2:47 PM"
  • • Vague terminology: Using "regarding" when you mean "primarily about"
  • • Missing date ranges: Open-ended requests get denied or delayed
